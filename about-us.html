About ShipsAll

Shipsall is all about you, the consumer shipper!
Our goal was to make shipping simple for everyone - not just business shippers.
So we designed a simple yet effective, and created some cool shipping tools for PC’s and mobile devices that allow you to save time and money. No more waiting in line!
We feel strongly about returning to the community, and for that reason donate a portion of the profit from each transaction to a non-profit orgnization of your choice.
Beyond community we are committed to our environment by ensuring the reduction of carbon emissions. All our shipping services include a Carbon Offset at no additional cost. We are the only national shipper whose services are all certified as Carbon Free by CarbonFund.org
We would love to hear from you if you have any ideas for new features you feel would enhance our product. E-mail us at: features@shipsall.com
